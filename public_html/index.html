Helping Keeping Your Children Safe

Smart Tracking Systems From Brick and Byte

When your children are at school, there’s some guarantee of safety. However most parents agree that it’s the trip there and back that worries them. The School Children Tracking System from Brick and Byte might allay their fears with its features for tracking movement and ensuring safety. With a “black box” installed in the bus, the sensors are put to use in determining the vehicle’s position, speed and some approximation of its behavior on the road. The logistical advantages also carry some weight with parents being able to coordinate pickups/dropos and plan routes or schedules accordingly.
